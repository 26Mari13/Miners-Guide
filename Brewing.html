Brewing Help

Need to learn how to make potions? Here's your guide to brewing in Minecraft. Where you will Learn the ingredients, steps, and effects of common potions.

Potion of Healing

Base Potion: Awkward Potion

Ingredient: Glistering Melon Slice

Effect: Instantly restores 4 hearts (8 HP).

Brewing Steps:

  1. Start with a Water Bottle in a Brewing Stand.
  2. Add Nether Wart to make an Awkward Potion.
  3. Add a Glistering Melon Slice.

Potion of Swiftness

Base Potion: Awkward Potion

Ingredient: Sugar

Effect: Increases movement speed by 20%.

Brewing Steps:

  1. Brew a Water Bottle with Nether Wart to make an Awkward Potion.
  2. Add Sugar to create Potion of Swiftness.

Potion of Fire Resistance

Base Potion: Awkward Potion

Ingredient: Magma Cream

Effect: Grants immunity to fire and lava damage.

Lasts: 4 minutes, 8 if you add redstone

Brewing Steps:

  1. Use Water Bottle + Nether Wart = Awkward Potion.
  2. Add Magma Cream to brew Fire Resistance.
