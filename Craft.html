Craft Help

Welcome to the Craft Help section! Below are some common crafting recipes:

Crafting Table

Ingredients:

  • 4 Wooden Planks

Crafted Item:

Crafting Table crafted from 4 Wooden Planks using your inventory crafting or using an actual crafting table.

Wooden sticks

Ingredients:

  • 2 Wooden Planks(Any wooden planks will do)

Crafted Item:

Wooden sticks crafted from 2 Wooden Planks using the crafting table or your inventory crafting table.

Ladders

Ingredients:

  • 7 sticks

Crafted Item:

Ladders crafted from 7 Sticks using the crafting table.

Item Frame

Ingredients:

  • 8 sticks
  • 1 Leather

Crafted Item:

Item Frame crafted from 8 Sticks and 1 Leather using the crafting table.

Ladders

Ingredients:

Torch

Ingredients:

Crafted Item:

Torch crafted from 1 stick and coal in crafting table or your inventory crafting table.

Chest

Ingredients:

Crafted Item:

Chest crafted from 8 planks in crafting table.

Fence

Fence crafted from 4 planks and 2 sticks in crafting table.
